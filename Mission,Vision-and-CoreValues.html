Aldersgate College Inc.

Vision

A 21st Century University
Transforming a global network
of learners for enlightenment,
leadership and human service.

Mission

Aldersgate College was established to develop
the power of reason, to liberate people from
ignorance, penury and hopelessness, and to
empower them to commence their own journey
for social and economic transformation and spiritual salvation.

Core Values

Academic Excellence
Train students to become academically
and professionally competent.

Social Relevance
Impresss norms, values and behaviors that
are socially relevant to the human development
needs of the majority and the less fortunate
and at the same time responsive to the ideals of a democratic society.

Spiritual Nurturance
Evolve and incubate spiritual knowingness
founded on absolute biblical truth and concurrently
promoting unity, moral uprightness and dedication
to Chistian Services.
